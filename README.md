## Overview

**Survive Burnout** is a chaotic, humor-driven workplace survival experience designed for Roblox.

Players enter an exaggerated corporate environment filled with impossible deadlines, unpredictable workplace disasters, demanding bosses, overloaded coworkers, malfunctioning office equipment, and increasingly absurd tasks.

To survive each workday, players must complete objectives, manage their time and energy, cooperate with coworkers, respond to chaos events, and prevent their **Burnout Meter** from reaching its limit.

Beneath its colorful and comedic presentation, the game explores themes of pressure, unrealistic expectations, unhealthy work culture, teamwork, emotional awareness, and the importance of balance.

The objective is not simply to work faster.

The objective is to survive the system without allowing it to consume you.

## The Design Challenge

Modern students and young professionals increasingly experience pressure from academic workloads, deadlines, internships, competition, unrealistic expectations, and productivity culture.

However, these experiences are rarely explored through accessible and entertaining interactive experiences.

Survive Burnout approaches the subject through:

- Workplace satire
- Exaggerated humor
- Fast and simple objectives
- Cooperative gameplay
- Resource-management decisions
- Unpredictable chaos events
- Meaningful player choices

Rather than presenting burnout as a lecture, the game transforms workplace pressure into an interactive system that players can experience, manage, and overcome together.

---

## Core Design Pillars

### 1. Controlled Chaos

Unexpected events constantly disrupt the workday.

Printers malfunction, systems fail, bosses panic, coworkers need assistance, deadlines change, and routine office tasks escalate into unpredictable situations.

The chaos should create funny and memorable moments without making objectives impossible to understand.

### 2. Simple Tasks, Meaningful Decisions

Individual tasks are designed to remain understandable for younger players.

The strategic depth comes from deciding:

- Which task should be completed first?
- Should the player work quickly and gain more burnout?
- Is taking a break worth losing time?
- Should a task be delegated?
- Should the player help a coworker?
- Is it safer to collaborate or continue alone?

### 3. Burnout as Gameplay

Burnout is not only a narrative theme. It is the primary gameplay system.

Player actions, deadlines, task pressure, workplace events, and interactions directly affect the Burnout Meter.

Managing burnout becomes as important as completing objectives.

### 4. Teamwork Over Isolation

Players are encouraged to:

- Share responsibilities
- Delegate tasks
- Help overwhelmed coworkers
- Respond to team emergencies
- Coordinate during chaos events

Effective cooperation can improve performance while reducing pressure on individual players.

### 5. Humor With Meaning

The workplace is intentionally exaggerated through absurd characters, dramatic emergencies, animated office equipment, chaotic events, and satirical corporate behavior.

The experience remains colorful, light-hearted, and accessible while communicating a meaningful theme.
